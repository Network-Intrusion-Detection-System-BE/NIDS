Ever vigilant, NIDS application safeguards your network. It examines both recorded traffic (pcap files) and live internet
activity, searching for patterns that indicate potential threats. By recognizing known malicious activity and spotting
unusual behavior, it acts as your network's first line of defense.



Need for NIDS

  • # NIDS provide in-depth visibility into what's happening on your network.
  • # By promptly identifying suspicious activity, NIDS can help you react to threats quicker, minimizing potential damage.
  • # Early detection of intrusions allows you to take action and stop them before they can cause significant disruption.


Benefits of NIDS

  • # NIDS constantly analyzes network traffic, allowing for immediate identification of suspicious activity.
  • # Compared to Host-based IDS (HIDS) that focus on individual devices, NIDS can monitor your entire network.
  • # Early detection of intrusions allows you to take action and stop them before they can cause significant disruption.


Functionalities of NIDS

  • # NIDS functions like a network sniffer, capturing all data packets flowing through the designated network segment.
  • # NIDS meticulously compare network traffic patterns to these signatures to identify suspicious events.
  • # NIDS meticulously log all network traffic data and detected events.
